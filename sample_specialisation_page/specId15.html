Foods To follow

The Diet to follow for three Trimster Only for Pregnant Women

First Trimster

Morning Sickness is very common – try some dry crackers or muri before leaving bed

Requires only 10kcal extra/day than your normal requirement; so don’t worry, only 1/2 tsp sugar or 1gm of butter can meet the extra needs.

Only 2.5kgwt gain is satisfactory

To cope up with heart – burn – avoid sweet foods, homemade curd with pinch of salt can give you relief.

No fruits or fruit juice in empty stomach.
