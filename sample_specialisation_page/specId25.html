Foods To follow

The Diet to follow for three weeks

Week 1

Breakfast
1/2 Grapefruit of juice
1 Slice of Toast
1 T Peanut Butter

Lunch
1/2 c Tuna
1 Slice Toast
Black Coffee or Tea

Dinner
2 Slices -3 oz of any meat
1 c String Beans
1 c Carrots of Beets
1 small Apple
1 c Vanilla Ice Cream 

Week 2

Breakfast
1 Hard Boiled Egg
1 Slice Toast
1/2 Banana
Black Coffee or Tea

Lunch
1 c Cottage Cheese or 1/2 c Tuna
5 Saltine Crackers

Dinner
2 Wieners
1 c Cabbage or Broccoli
1/2 c Carrots or Turnip Greens
1/2 Banana
1 c Vanilla Ice Cream

Week 3

Breakfast
1 Hard Boiled Egg
1 Slice Toast
Black Coffee or Tea

Lunch
1 Slice Cheddar Cheese
5 Saltine Crackers
1 small apple
Black Coffee or Tea

Dinner
1 c Tuna
1 c Carrots or Beets
1 c Green Beans or Cauliflower
1 c Cantaloupe or small Apple
1 c Vanilla ice Cream
